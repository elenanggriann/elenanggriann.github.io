Project information

This project is part of the final project of the Kewirausahaan course which requires students to open a small business. Not only that, students are also required to design a marketing strategy. One of the marketing strategies implemented is marketing through social media.

The Instagram feed design consists of 3 sections. The first section is the announcement section where Gimindo products will be launched soon. The second part is the first launch of gimindo products. The third part is the announcement of the opening of Gimindo pre-order.
